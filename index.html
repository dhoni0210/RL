The Story of Reena's Laundry


Started in 2025, Reena's Laundry began with a simple mission: to give families and busy professionals their weekends back. We realized how much time people spent washing, drying, and ironing clothes—time that could be spent with loved ones or resting.


What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a trusted local business. We treat every garment as if it were our own, using eco-friendly detergents, state-of-the-art steam presses, and a rigorous quality check process.


Our Promise: Reliability, Affordability, and Care. When you hand over your laundry to us, you are guaranteed fresh, clean clothes delivered on time, every time.
